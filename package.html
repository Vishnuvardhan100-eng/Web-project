ABOUT This Site

Gadisar Lake, Jaisalmer, Rajasthan

In Jaisalmer, Rajasthan, Gadisar Lake is a properly-favoured traveller vacation spot that provides a tranquil haven from the scorching barren region sun. Maharawal Gadsi Singh constructed this synthetic reservoir in the 14th century to supply water to the parched place With lovely temples, shrines, and ghats all around, the lake is the ideal spot to unwind and soak up Jaisalmer's rich past and lifestyle.
