ABOUT This Site

Srirangham Temple

The Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple in Srirangam, Tamil Nadu, is special for being the largest functioning Hindu temple in the world, a significant Vaishnavite temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu, and one of the 108 Divya Desams (holy shrines). This Temple in Srirangam, Tamil Nadu, has a history spanning over 2,000 years, with evidence of its existence dating back to the 1st century CE during the Sangam period.
